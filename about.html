Why "KAPEling"?

The name "KAPEling" is an idea that dates back to the owner, Mr. Maverick Serencio's college days. "Kape", meaning "coffee" added with the suffix "ling" which when placed together sounds like "kapiling", a Filipino word meaning "together" which reminds Mr. Maverick of his roots and origin of his love for coffee. He and his friends would go out and bond over a cold cup of iced coffee in which they all love having together. This also explains the meaning behind the logo which is two coffee beans beside each other forming a heart, symbolizing love for the culture and the experience.

Where We Started

Our journey began with a passion for sourcing the finest coffee beans from around the world and trying to share that sweet experience that we had to our beloved patrons who are just like us, trying to find the meaning of life over a cup of coffee.

Our founder, Jason Maverick Serencio, envisioned creating a space for lost souls, not just coffee enthusiasts where they could come together to enjoy the rich flavors and aromas of meticulously crafted brews and top-of-the-line pastries.

Today, KAPEling continues to uphold its commitment to quality and community, offering a diverse menu of coffee creations to delight our customers.
